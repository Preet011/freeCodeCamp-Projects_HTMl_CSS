Recycled plastic coffee tables

Materials

The choice of materials is an essential step in our mission to make sustainable furniture. We use very robust materials that are either already recycled or easy-to-recycle.

Care Instructions

The wood is protected by a water-based varnish and does not require any special cleaning products. A damp sponge and a soft cloth are sufficient. The structure made of powder-coated steel is built to last and does not require any special maintenance. If necessary, you can clean it with a microfibre cloth and clean water.

Shipping

We make sure you recieve your furniture as soon as we have finished making it. We also provide free returns if you are not satisfied.
