Maharaja Ranjit Singh

Sher-e-Panjab

Here's a time line of Maharaja Ranjit Singh's life:

  • 1776-Maharaja Ranjit Singh, famously called ‘Sher-e-Panjab’ (Lion of Panjab) was known to be a fierce king was born at Gujranwala (now in Pakistan)
  • 1799 -Singh captured Lahore which is also considered as the turning point of the Sikh Empire.
  • 1801-He was crowned Maharaja at the age of 20
  • 1809 -Ranjit Singh captured Kangra
  • 1813 -Ranjit Singh captured Attock
  • 1818 -Ranjit Singh captured Multan
  • 1819-He conquered Kashmir
  • 1820-Ranjit Singh began to modernize his army, using European officers—many of whom had served in the army of Napoleon I—to train the infantry and the artillery.
  • 1831-Ranjit Singh met with British officials regarding the disposition of Sindh province (now in southeastern Pakistan)
  • 1839-Maharaja Ranjit Singh passed away in Lahore Fort
  • 1838-He agreed to a treaty with the British viceroy Lord Auckland to restore Shah Shojāʿ to the Afghan throne at Kabul
